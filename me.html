me

hihi welcome to my website :) My name is technically Narawutti Wongwandanee but for everyone's ease I go by Brahm.

I was born in Bangkok, Thailand and I moved to Calgary, Canada, which is where I grew up with my parents and my older brother. Right now I'm studying in a combined major for Microbiology and Computer Science at the University of British Columbia!!

This site is just a fun personal project for me coded with HTML and CSS using jekyll and my own theme! I love drawing, rhythm games, gunpla and going to the gym :> I also watch anime sometimes. Maybe when im rich i want to travel more.
